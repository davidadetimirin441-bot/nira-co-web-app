NIRA & CO email verification setup

Why verification did not arrive:
- The local app only sends real email when SMTP variables are configured.
- If SMTP is missing, the app saves the verification/reset email in the database email_outbox table.

Local setup:
1. Copy .env.example to .env inside this folder.
2. Set these values:
   BASE_URL=http://127.0.0.1:8088
   SECRET_KEY=use-a-long-random-value-and-keep-it-private
   COOKIE_SECURE=0
   SMTP_HOST=smtp.gmail.com
   SMTP_PORT=587
   SMTP_USERNAME=your-gmail-address@gmail.com
   SMTP_PASSWORD=your-google-app-password
   FROM_EMAIL=your-gmail-address@gmail.com
   SMTP_TLS=1

Gmail note:
- Use a Google App Password, not your normal Gmail password.
- Your Google account usually needs 2-Step Verification enabled before App Passwords are available.

After editing .env:
1. Restart the app with start-nira.bat.
2. Register a new account or click Resend verification email.
3. Check your inbox and spam folder.

Local outbox check:
Run:
  python show_outbox.py

That prints the latest saved verification/reset emails from the local database.
